# TERMS & CONDITIONS - APPOINTMENT BOOKING WITH PAYMENT

**Effective Date**: January 28, 2026

## 1. Platform Role and Services

### 1.1 Technology Provider
Nrome ("the Platform", "we", "us", "our") is a technology service that facilitates appointment bookings between patients and independent medical practitioners. The Platform provides:
- Online appointment scheduling tools
- Secure payment processing infrastructure
- Communication and notification services
- Data storage and management systems

### 1.2 No Medical Services
**The Platform does NOT**:
- Provide medical services, advice, diagnosis, or treatment
- Make clinical decisions or recommendations
- Act as a medical scheme, insurer, or health provider
- Employ or directly supervise medical practitioners
- Guarantee medical outcomes or results

### 1.3 Independent Practitioners
All medical practitioners listed on the Platform:
- Act independently and autonomously
- Are solely responsible for their medical services
- Maintain their own professional licenses and registrations
- Are individually liable for clinical decisions and care provided
- Set their own consultation fees and availability

## 2. Payments and Fees

### 2.1 Payment Structure
When you book an appointment, you pay a single fee that includes:
- **Consultation Fee**: The practitioner's professional fee
- **Platform Service Fee**: Technology and infrastructure costs
- **Payment Processing Fee**: Secure transaction handling

**Example**: For a R500.00 appointment:
- Total charged to patient: R500.00
- Practitioner receives: R400.00 (80%)
- Platform service fee: R100.00 (20%)

### 2.2 Fee Transparency
The total amount includes all fees. The breakdown is:
- **Clearly displayed** before payment
- **Non-negotiable** once accepted
- **Includes processing fees** for secure payment handling
- **Covers platform services** including appointment management, reminders, and support

### 2.3 Payment Methods
We accept payments via Paystack, which supports:
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Bank transfers
- Mobile money (where available)

### 2.4 Payment Security
- All payments processed through Paystack (PCI-DSS compliant)
- Your card details are never stored on our servers
- Transactions are encrypted and secure
- Payment confirmation is sent immediately

### 2.5 Split Payments
Payments are automatically split at the time of transaction:
- **80% transferred directly** to the practitioner's bank account
- **20% retained** by the platform for service fees
- **No pooled funds** - all splits occur automatically
- **Transparent audit trail** maintained for all transactions

## 3. Appointment Booking Process

### 3.1 Booking Flow
1. **Patient selects** practitioner, date, and time
2. **Patient enters** contact details and payment information
3. **Payment is processed** - appointment status: PENDING_PAYMENT
4. **Payment successful** - appointment status: PENDING_CONFIRMATION
5. **Practitioner confirms** (within 24 hours) - status: CONFIRMED
6. **Appointment occurs** - status: COMPLETED

### 3.2 Confirmation Requirement
- Practitioners have **24 hours** to confirm or decline appointments
- You will be notified via email when confirmed
- If not confirmed within 24 hours, **automatic full refund** is issued
- This protects you from unconfirmed appointments

### 3.3 Appointment Reminders
We will send reminder notifications:
- **24 hours before** your appointment
- Via email and/or SMS (if provided)
- Free of charge as part of our service

## 4. Cancellation and Refund Policy

### 4.1 Patient Cancellations

#### 4.1.1 Early Cancellation (≥24 Hours Before Appointment)
- **Full refund** of all fees paid (R500.00 in example)
- Refund processed within **5-10 business days**
- No cancellation fee charged
- Practitioner and platform fees fully refunded

#### 4.1.2 Late Cancellation (<24 Hours Before Appointment)
- **No refund** issued (no-show policy applies)
- This compensates the practitioner for reserved time
- Clearly disclosed before payment
- Exceptions may be granted for emergencies (at our discretion)

### 4.2 Practitioner Cancellations

#### 4.2.1 Before Confirmation
- Practitioner declines within 24 hours
- **Full automatic refund** issued
- No fees retained by anyone
- You are notified immediately

#### 4.2.2 After Confirmation
- **Full refund** issued immediately
- Optional **goodwill credit** for inconvenience
- We may provide assistance finding alternative practitioner
- Practitioner may face penalties for repeated cancellations

### 4.3 System-Initiated Refunds

#### 4.3.1 Unconfirmed Appointments
- Practitioner doesn't respond within 24 hours
- **Automatic full refund** triggered by system
- No action required from you
- Refund notification sent automatically

#### 4.3.2 Technical Issues
- Payment processed but appointment not created
- Platform technical failures
- **Full refund** issued immediately
- Incident investigated and resolved

### 4.4 Refund Processing
- Refunds processed via **Paystack** to original payment method
- Timeframe: **5-10 business days** (bank-dependent)
- Refund confirmation email sent
- Full audit trail maintained for your records

### 4.5 No Partial Refunds
- Refunds are **all-or-nothing** (0% or 100%)
- No partial refunds for late arrivals or shortened appointments
- Disputes handled through our resolution process

## 5. No-Show Policy

### 5.1 Patient No-Show
If you fail to attend a confirmed appointment:
- **No refund** will be issued
- Full payment retained (**R500.00** in example)
- Practitioner compensated for reserved time
- Repeated no-shows may result in account restrictions

### 5.2 No-Show Fee Breakdown
- **80% to practitioner** (R400.00) - compensation for time
- **20% to platform** (R100.00) - administrative costs

### 5.3 Grace Period
- **15-minute grace period** for late arrivals
- After 15 minutes, practitioner may mark as no-show
- Communicate with practitioner if running late

### 5.4 Disputing No-Shows
If you attended but were marked no-show:
- Contact support within **48 hours** with proof
- Provide appointment confirmation and evidence
- We will investigate with the practitioner
- Refund issued if attendance is verified
