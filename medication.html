Terms & Conditions
1. Service Agreement

By using our medication home delivery service, you agree to provide accurate prescription and medical information.

2. Prescription Verification

All prescriptions will be verified by a licensed pharmacist before processing. Invalid or expired prescriptions will be rejected.

3. Delivery Terms

We aim to deliver medications within the specified timeframe. Delays may occur due to weather, traffic, or verification issues.

4. Payment

Payment is required before delivery unless using insurance coverage. Refunds are subject to our return policy.

5. Patient Responsibility

You must provide accurate allergy and medical history information. We are not liable for adverse reactions due to incomplete information.

6. Prescription Accuracy

Please verify all medication details upon delivery. Report any discrepancies immediately.

7. Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled before pharmacist verification. Post-verification cancellations may incur charges.

Privacy Policy
Data Collection

We collect personal information (name, contact details), medical information (allergies, conditions, current medications), and prescription data to provide our services.

Data Usage

Your information is used solely for:

  • Processing and delivering medication orders
  • Verifying prescriptions with healthcare providers
  • Ensuring medication safety and preventing drug interactions
  • Communicating order status and delivery updates
  • Processing insurance claims where applicable
Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your sensitive medical information. All data is encrypted and stored securely.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is shared only with:

  • Licensed pharmacists for prescription verification
  • Healthcare providers for prescription validation
  • Insurance providers for claims processing (with your consent)
  • Delivery partners for order fulfillment (limited information only)
HIPAA Compliance

We maintain strict confidentiality of your health information in accordance with healthcare privacy regulations.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact our support team for assistance.

Data Retention

We retain prescription and medical records as required by law (typically 7 years). Inactive accounts may be archived after 2 years.
